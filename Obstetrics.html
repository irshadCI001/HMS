Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department

The Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department at Infinity Care is dedicated to providing holistic and patient-centered care for women of all ages. From adolescence to menopause, our team supports every phase of a woman’s reproductive health journey. We specialize in managing pregnancies — including high-risk cases — and offer advanced gynecological procedures using the latest technology and compassionate care.

Services

  • Prenatal and Postnatal Care
  • High-Risk Pregnancy Management
  • Ultrasound and Fetal Monitoring
  • Gynecological Surgeries
  • Menstrual and Hormonal Disorder Treatment
  • Menopause Management
  • Contraceptive Counseling
