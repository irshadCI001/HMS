Radiology Department

The Radiology Department at Infinity Care provides comprehensive diagnostic imaging services utilizing the latest technology. From routine X-rays to advanced MRIs and image-guided procedures, our experienced radiologists ensure precise interpretations for accurate medical decision-making. Our department plays a critical role in both preventive and emergency care.

Services

  • X-ray and Ultrasound Imaging
  • CT Scan and MRI
  • Digital Mammography
  • Interventional Radiology Procedures
  • Image-Guided Biopsies
  • Fluoroscopy and Contrast Studies
