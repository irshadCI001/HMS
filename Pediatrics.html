Pediatrics Department

The Pediatrics Department at Infinity Care is devoted to the physical, emotional, and social well-being of infants, children, and adolescents. From routine check-ups and immunizations to the treatment of complex medical conditions, our expert pediatricians provide gentle and attentive care in a child-friendly environment. We focus on both preventive measures and timely interventions to ensure healthy development at every stage.

Services

  • Newborn and Infant Care
  • Childhood Immunizations
  • Growth and Development Monitoring
  • Pediatric Emergencies
  • Nutritional and Behavioral Counseling
  • Asthma and Allergy Management
  • Child Mental Health Support
