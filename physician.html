General Physician Department

The General Physician Department at Infinity Care is your first point of contact for non-emergency health issues. Our experienced physicians provide holistic care, preventive screenings, chronic disease management, and health education tailored for patients of all ages.

Services

  • Management of Diabetes and Hypertension
  • Infectious Disease Treatment
  • Preventive Screenings and Vaccinations
  • Diet and Lifestyle Counseling
  • Minor Procedures and Wound Care
  • Referrals to Specialists when needed
