Anesthesiology Department

The Anesthesiology Department at Infinity Care ensures patient safety and comfort before, during, and after surgical procedures. Our team of skilled anesthesiologists uses the latest techniques and monitoring systems to provide personalized anesthesia care and pain management services.

Services

  • Pre-operative Anesthetic Assessment
  • General and Regional Anesthesia
  • Sedation for Minor Procedures
  • Pain Management Consultations
  • Critical Care and Emergency Anesthesia
  • Postoperative Recovery Monitoring
  • Obstetric Anesthesia for Painless Deliveries
